How to maintain your account

Add money to your account any time to keep your account on and uninterrupted.

Make sure to add sufficient funds in your account to cover your daily subscription amount. The daily charge will be deducted automatically from your Boost Mobile account after midnight (based on your time zone) each day, regardless of usage.

Recharge at least once every 90 days to keep your account active (after 90 days any credit balance will expire). Once you hit a zero credit balance, you have 60 days to add money and keep your service and account active.

How to add money to you account

Adding money to your account is easy. Choose the method that works best for you.

Dial #ADD (#233) to add money from your handset or visit My Account online. You can also securely register your preferred way to pay online to make faster one-time payments anytime.

You can also pay in person with cash or credit at one of thousands of authorized retail locations.

How to use Auto Re-Boost™

Once you've registered a credit/debit card or account, you can set up automatic payments. It's called Auto Re-Boost™ and it's the easiest way for you to keep your service active.

Sign up for Auto Re-Boost™, and Boost® will automatically process your monthly payment every month on your monthly payment date.

How to check your balance

Checking your balance is quick and painless.

Dial #BAL (#225) from your handset to check your balance for FREE.

You can also check your monthly payment date (for Monthly customers) and add funds to your account with your registered way to pay.

Get more high-speed data.

Add a data pack to your monthly plan.

With data packs, you're always ready for a data binge. Any time you need more 3G/4G data, just add a data pack so you can keep streaming, gaming and surfing at high speed. The data pack will give you some extra data for the month.

Add to My Account

Here's how it works:

  • A data pack is separate from the data included in your monthly service plan.
  • The data in the data pack add-on will be used before the high-speed data in your monthly service plan, should it be available.
  • Use your data pack within 30 days of purchase.
  • Data packs are a one-time purchase. If you use all the data in your data pack before the 30 days are up, you can purchase an additional data pack.
